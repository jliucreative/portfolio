Her name was Elizabeth Short...

She was an aspiring actress located in Los Angeles.

Described as a "pretty girl with blue eyes..."

she would spend her nights drinking with her friends and days working as a waitress.

After becoming widowed, Elizabeth found herself partying with a new man, Robert Manly.

After spending time together at a party, Manly dropped Short off at a hotel before she was to return to Massechusettes.

She was reported missing on January 9, 1947.
